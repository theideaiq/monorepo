## 2024-11-20 - Missing Product Structured Data
**Learning:** Product detail pages were missing JSON-LD Structured Data for Products, which is critical for search engines to understand the product entity and enable rich snippets in search results.
**Action:** When creating or maintaining e-commerce product pages, always inject `Product` Schema.org JSON-LD to expose key attributes like name, image, description, sku, brand, offers (price, availability), and aggregate ratings to crawlers.
