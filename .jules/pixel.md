## 2026-02-27 - [Dark Mode Card Styling] **Learning:** The design token `bg-brand-surface` (#1e1e1e) is the correct standard for dark theme card backgrounds, replacing ad-hoc hex values like `#1e1e1e` or `bg-white/5`. However, simply applying the background can make cards look flat. **Action:** Pair `bg-brand-surface` with `hover:shadow-lg` and a colored shadow (e.g., `shadow-brand-pink/20`) derived from the card's accent color to create depth and interactivity without breaking the dark mode aesthetic.
